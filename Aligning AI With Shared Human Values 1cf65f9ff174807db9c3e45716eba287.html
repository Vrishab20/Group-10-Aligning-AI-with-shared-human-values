Benchmarking Qwen 2.5 M

The goal is to bench mark Qwen LLM against the Ethics dataset (a compilation of questions).

I will be conducting multiple sets of experiments: I will try to craft
multiple prompts that leverage different prompt engineering techniques. Given that llms have a token limit I will see if splitting the datasets into mini batches can help mitigate the issue. At the end, I will analyze the results for patterns or insights.
